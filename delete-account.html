Delete Your Data

For the Lines of Action app by Vlepa Apps · Updated June 17, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of the data associated with your use of the Lines of Action mobile game, published by Vlepa Apps.

Lines of Action does not require you to create an account — the optional online multiplayer mode assigns your device an anonymous ID, and we never collect your real name, email address, or password. If you have used online multiplayer, you can still request deletion of the anonymous profile and gameplay data tied to your device.

How to request deletion

  1. Open the App and go to Online → your profile. At the bottom of your profile card you will find your Account ID — tap it to copy it.
  2. Send an email to info@vlepa.app with the subject "Delete my data (Lines of Action)".
  3. Paste your Account ID into the email. We use it to verify the request comes from the account owner — only you can see your Account ID, on your own device.
  4. We will process your request and confirm by reply. No account login is required.

📧 Quick link: info@vlepa.app — subject "Delete my data (Lines of Action)", and include your Account ID from the App (Online → your profile).

What data is deleted

Upon your request, we permanently delete all data tied to your online profile in Google Firebase, including:

  • Your anonymous user ID
  • Your randomly generated display name and chosen avatar
  • Match history and win/loss statistics
  • Approximate country code (derived from device language)
  • Online presence (online/offline status)
  • Any reports or blocks you created

What data is kept

The following is not tied to your identity and is not part of your online profile:

  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics (e.g., overall feature usage) collected via Firebase Analytics, retained under Google's data retention policies. This data cannot be used to identify you.
  • Advertising identifiers managed by your device and Google AdMob — you can reset or limit these in your device settings at any time.

Timing & retention

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Your profile and gameplay data are removed from our live database immediately upon processing, and purged from routine backups within 90 days. After deletion, the data cannot be recovered.

Contact

Questions about data deletion or privacy? Email us at info@vlepa.app. See also our Privacy Policy.
